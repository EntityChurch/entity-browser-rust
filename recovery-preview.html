What is this? Entity Browser is a local-first application: your content and your identity live in an entity tree stored inside this browser — not on a remote server. Each identity is a peer; the tree is the single source of truth, and the app works offline because everything it needs is already on your machine. This page is a read-only inventory of everything Entity Browser has saved here.

What "here" means — scope. Browsers partition storage by origin = protocol + host + port (this one is …). Everything below belongs to that origin. The URL path does not matter: every page on this origin and every open tab share the same storage shown here. A different port or domain is a completely separate world with its own independent data.
